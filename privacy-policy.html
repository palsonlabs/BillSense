BillSense Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2025

Privacy First: BillSense is designed with your privacy as our top priority. All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Information We Collect

BillSense operates entirely offline. Your data never leaves your device.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Bill Information: Bill names, amounts, due dates, payment status, and notes
  • Income Information: Income sources, amounts, frequencies, and payment schedules
  • Bank Account Names: Account nicknames for organization (no actual bank connections)
  • App Preferences: Display settings, notification preferences, currency selection
  • Security Settings: App Lock PIN (encrypted and stored securely by iOS)
  • Category Customizations: Custom expense categories and colors

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ No personally identifiable information
  • ❌ No location tracking
  • ❌ No usage analytics or telemetry
  • ❌ No contact information
  • ❌ No device identifiers
  • ❌ No crash reports with personal data
  • ❌ No advertising identifiers

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes within the app:

Core Functionality

  • Display Bills: Show your bills, payment schedules, and financial overview
  • Send Reminders: Create local notifications for upcoming bill due dates (only if you enable notifications)
  • Track Payments: Record payment history and status
  • Provide Insights: Generate charts and analytics from your local data

Security

  • App Lock: Protect your financial data using Face ID, Touch ID, or PIN
  • Biometric Authentication: Used solely for unlocking the app (processed entirely on-device by iOS)

Data Storage & Security

100% Local Storage: All your data is stored on your device using Apple's secure UserDefaults system, which is encrypted by iOS at the operating system level.

Our Security Measures

  • ✅ All data encrypted by iOS automatically
  • ✅ Optional App Lock with biometric or PIN protection
  • ✅ No network transmission of financial data
  • ✅ No cloud storage or external backups (unless you use iCloud backup at the device level)
  • ✅ No third-party access to your data

Third-Party Services

Apple StoreKit

BillSense uses Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases and subscriptions. When you make a purchase:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple
  • We do not see or store your payment information
  • We receive only a subscription status indicator (active/inactive)
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions

Apple Notifications

If you enable notifications, BillSense uses Apple's local notification system:

  • Notifications are generated locally on your device
  • No notification data is sent to external servers
  • You control notification permissions in iOS Settings

Your Rights & Control

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

Access Your Data

  • All your data is visible within the app at all times
  • No hidden data collection

Export Your Data

  • Pro users can export data to CSV format
  • Your data, your format

Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual bills, categories, or income sources at any time
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data from your device
  • No data remains on external servers (because we don't use any)

Modify Your Data

  • Edit or update any information at any time
  • No waiting period or approval required

Children's Privacy

BillSense is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The app is designed for adults managing their personal finances.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the app is installed. When you uninstall BillSense, all data is automatically removed from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

International Users

BillSense operates entirely on your device and does not transfer data internationally. Your data never leaves your device, regardless of your location.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents: BillSense does not sell your personal information. We do not collect personal information as defined by the CCPA, as all data remains local to your device.

European Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Union: BillSense complies with GDPR principles by design:

  • ✅ Data minimization: We collect only what's necessary for app functionality
  • ✅ Purpose limitation: Data is used only for stated purposes
  • ✅ Storage limitation: Data is stored only on your device
  • ✅ Right to access: Full access to all your data within the app
  • ✅ Right to erasure: Delete data or uninstall app
